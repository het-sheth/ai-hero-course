## Lesson page content

One of the most useful resources during the cohort is the **Discord server** — a place to get
questions answered, have discussions, and talk about stuff out of scope for the cohort or things
discovered while going through the material.

### Community Channels
Channels not related to the course; anyone in the community can access these (even without buying a
course):
- `#general` — general chat
- `#ai-hero` — stuff specific to AI Hero
- `#evalite` — one of the prominent open source repositories
- `#sandcastle` — another open source repository
- `#skills` — the skills repository
- `#showcase` — for people to showcase whatever they want
- `#random` — for random stuff

### Cohort Channels
Once you have your cohort role, you'll see the `#ai-coding-cohort` category with exclusive channels.
If you have the **Cohort 004** role you can see these. Previous cohort members with the **Cohort 003**
role can also access all of these, including the Cohort 004-specific ones.

| Channel | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `#ai-coding-cohort-general` | For anyone who's taken the AI coding cohort. General announcements and course-related chat. |
| `#ai-coding-cohort-random` | Out of scope and off-topic stuff for cohort members. |
| `#cohort-004-questions` | The most important channel. Ask questions about specific things inside the course. Open a thread to get help with setup or anything else. |
| `#cohort-004-office-hours-questions` | Questions you want answered specifically during office hours. The best/most popular ones get floated to the top. |

### How to Get Help
For any questions regarding setup or anything else, open a thread inside `#cohort-004-questions`.
During office hours, Matt answers questions, looking at `#cohort-004-office-hours-questions` to see
what people want to discuss.

### Heroes and Participation
Matt sits in the Discord answering questions throughout the cohort, and **nominates "heroes"** —
people who are really helpful, post interesting tips, or generally show up and answer folks'
questions. Heroes get a red hero role. If you see heroes, thank them for their generosity.

### Troubleshooting Access
If you can't see the channels, click the link in the lesson, which takes you to an interactive setup
to recover the role you've purchased.
