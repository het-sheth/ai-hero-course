The Discord server is the most useful support resource during the cohort: a place to get questions
answered, have discussions, and talk about things out of scope for the course (or things you
discover while working through the material).

## Community channels (open to everyone)

These aren't tied to the course — anyone in the community can access them, even without buying a
course:

| Channel | What it's for |
|---------|---------------|
| `#general` | General chat |
| `#ai-hero` | Stuff specific to AI Hero |
| `#evalite` | One of the prominent open-source repos |
| `#sandcastle` | Another open-source repo |
| `#skills` | The skills repository |
| `#showcase` | Showcase whatever you want |
| `#random` | Random stuff |

## Cohort channels (need the cohort role)

Once you have your cohort role you'll see the `#ai-coding-cohort` category. The **Cohort 004** role
unlocks these; **Cohort 003** members can also see all of them, including the Cohort 004-specific
ones.

| Channel | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `#ai-coding-cohort-general` | General announcements and course chat for anyone who's taken the cohort |
| `#ai-coding-cohort-random` | Out-of-scope, off-topic stuff for cohort members |
| `#cohort-004-questions` | **The most important channel.** Open a thread to get help with setup or anything inside the course |
| `#cohort-004-office-hours-questions` | Questions you want answered during office hours — the best/most popular get floated to the top |

## How to get help

For setup or anything you need answered, open a thread in `#cohort-004-questions`. During office
hours Matt answers questions, watching `#cohort-004-office-hours-questions` for what people want to
discuss.

> [!TIP] Heroes
> Matt nominates **"heroes"** — people who are really helpful, post interesting tips, or generally
> show up and answer questions. Heroes get a red hero role. If you see one, thank them.

> [!NOTE] Can't see the channels?
> Click the link in the lesson to reach an interactive setup that recovers the role you purchased.
