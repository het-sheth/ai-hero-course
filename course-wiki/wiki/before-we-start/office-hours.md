Matt runs **three live office-hours streams** during the two-week cohort — about 30–45 minutes each,
on **YouTube Live**. Join and comment with your YouTube account and he'll see it live. Anything's on
the table: course questions or whatever came up while you were learning.

> [!TIP] Not mandatory — mostly for vibes
> All sessions are recorded as **YouTube VODs**, and any interesting topics that come up usually get
> re-recorded as standalone explainers in the course. So there's no need to watch the whole thing —
> but if you want to walk through something in depth, office hours is the time.
