Where We're Going (01.01)

Intro to the cohort and the overall approach. Big idea: own your process — rather than adopting heavy frameworks (GSD, Spec Kit, BMAD), learn a process you can iterate on and make your own, grounded in real engineering skills.

  • The Seven-Phase Process — Grill → Research → Prototype → PRD → Issues → Implement → Review; phase 6 runs AFK so you can parallelize

Also covered: there's a ~20,000-line playground app (TypeScript / Node / React) to build real features in; I can use it or apply the exercises to my own repo. Recommendation is the cohort project for fuller support — which is what I forked.
