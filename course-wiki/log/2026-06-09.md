## What I covered

Finished the **Getting to Know Claude Code** primer (02.03–02.07). Archived each lesson's transcript
under `raw/gtk-claude-code/` and built a page per lesson:

- [[gtk-claude-code/prompting-in-the-terminal|Prompting in the Terminal]] (02.03) — `@` to reference files into context, `Ctrl+S` to stash a prompt (and `Ctrl+C` to drop it), pasting images.
- [[gtk-claude-code/claude-and-your-ide|Claude & Your IDE]] (02.04) — `/ide` to connect the editor; the real payoff is rich, reviewable diffs in the IDE instead of the terminal.
- [[gtk-claude-code/going-forwards-and-backwards-in-time|Going Forwards & Backwards in Time]] (02.05) — double-`Esc` rewind mode (restore code and/or conversation); sessions persist locally, resume with `/resume` or `claude --continue`.
- [[gtk-claude-code/running-bash-commands|Running Bash Commands]] (02.06) — `!` bash mode (output into Claude's context), `Ctrl+B` to background a dev server, `Ctrl+Z`/`fg` to suspend Claude and hide commands from it.
- [[gtk-claude-code/permissions|Permissions]] (02.07) — strict-by-default approve/deny model, `.claude/settings.local.json` allow/deny + wildcards, sharing via `settings.json`, and web-fetch permissions.
